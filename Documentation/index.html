Installation

Step#1 Unpack the Script
Step#2 Upload the database.sql to PHPMyadmin.
Step#3 Change the .env in root folder with your database credientials and Change the APP_DEMO to false in Live Server.
Step#4 Change the Permission 777 to storage, public/photos, public/resume folders.
Step#5 Instllation Complete!.

Administator Login HR Administrator Portal

Dashboard

It used for display the system statistics like total employees, total time worked, total salary paid and top performaners graphical chart. It also displays the current day attendance log.

Employees

In this section you can view/edit employees and also appoint new employees for the particular position with their Photo & Resume. Administrator can also download the resume.

Projects

This section used for Project Management which is using for add/edit projects and also update the project status

Leave

Here you can access the Leave Requests from Employees and approve/decline the leave requests.

Timesheet

SmartHRM offers you a unique tool that can track every second of your employees. Here you can find total logs of all employee's timesheets and update/delete the entries.

Attendance

Attendance is one of the important factor in smart hrm which is using the attendance tracking of employees in table and calendar view. Here also you can track the guest visits too!

Departments & Postings

This section is used to manage Departments like Administration, Financial, HR and the Postings like Administrator, Accountant, Manager things. You can also view the company hierarchy view.

Payroll

Payroll which is most important factor in HR software. You can generate Payroll Statements and get printouts of Salary Slips here. Also can view/edit the transactions which is made for salary.

Settings

Smart HRM offers complete customization without any coding skills. You can update the tax%, deduction% and the company details from this settings menu.

Profile

You can change/update the Administror credientials here.
